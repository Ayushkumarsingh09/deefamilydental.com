Meet Dr. Anthony Dee

A Dentist Who Understands Family

Indiana National Guard — Major DMD, Southern Illinois University Father of Six

Dr. Anthony Dee isn't just your dentist — he's a husband, father of six, and a Major in the Indiana National Guard who serves in the Medical Support Unit. He also holds credentials at the Indianapolis VA Hospital, proudly serving both his community and his country.

After earning his biology degree from Indiana University and his DMD from Southern Illinois University School of Dental Medicine, Dr. Dee completed his General Practice Residency at Indiana University. He brought that expertise home to Columbus, where he built a practice rooted in honesty, compassion, and long-term relationships.

When he's not caring for patients, you'll find Dr. Dee gardening, staying fit, or training for triathlons — always alongside his wife, six children, two dogs, and yes, even a cat he's allergic to.

"I treat every patient the way I'd want my own family treated — with honesty, gentleness, and the highest standard of care."
